1. **User Management**  
   - Functionality: Handles user registration, authentication, and profile management, allowing users to create accounts and manage their preferences.  

2. **Music Streaming**  
   - Functionality: Provides users with access to millions of music tracks, including features to play, pause, and skip songs in real-time.  

3. **Playlist Management**  
   - Functionality: Allows users to create, edit, and share personal playlists, enabling them to curate their listening experience.  

4. **Search and Discovery**  
   - Functionality: Enables users to search for music by genres, artists, or albums and discover new tracks based on personalized recommendations.  

5. **Recommendation System**  
   - Functionality: Utilizes machine learning algorithms to analyze user preferences and suggest new music tailored to individual tastes.  

6. **Offline Access**  
   - Functionality: Allows users to download tracks for offline listening, ensuring that they can enjoy music without an internet connection.  

7. **Podcast and Radio**  
   - Functionality: Supports streaming of podcasts and radio stations, providing a broader range of audio content beyond just music.  

8. **Content Management for Artists**  
   - Functionality: Provides tools for artists to upload and manage their exclusive releases on the platform.
