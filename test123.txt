1. **User Management**  
   - Functionality: Handles user registration, authentication, and profile management, allowing users to create and maintain their accounts.

2. **Music Discovery**  
   - Functionality: Provides users with personalized playlist creation, genre searches, and music recommendations based on listening preferences through machine learning algorithms.

3. **Content Management**  
   - Functionality: Allows users to create and share playlists, as well as manage and download tracks for offline listening.

4. **Podcast and Radio Integration**  
   - Functionality: Supports access to podcasts, radio stations, and exclusive artist releases, enhancing the content variety available to users.

5. **Recommendation System**  
   - Functionality: Employs algorithms to analyze user preferences and provide tailored music recommendations, improving user engagement and satisfaction.
